Feria Nacional Potosina (FENAPO)

About Feria Nacional Potosina FENAPO

The National Potosina Fair (FENAPO) is one of the most important events in the state, it is planned many months in advance and is held at the Fairgrounds of the capital during the summer.
The entertainment proposal of FENAPO, designed for all audiences, is undoubtedly its greatest attraction. That is why it conquers thousands of people from different parts of the state, and from other regions of the country.
First of all, the list of guest artists that perform every midnight in the Palenque, or in its renovated Teatro del Pueblo (People's Theatre), which is now roofed and the shows there are completely free.
In both scenarios, consolidated stars such as Alejandro Fernández, Los Tigres del Norte, Los Ángeles Azules and the singer Lila Downs have been presented, just to name a few.
Also outstanding are the agri-food and livestock exhibitions, skirmishes, rodeo, bullfights, cock palenque, the spectacle of the Papantla flying and, of course, the mechanical games, one of the favorite spaces of the youngest.
Not forgetting the extensive program of cultural, artistic and sport events that seek to exalt the culture, flavor and tradition of the Potosi entity over time.
So now you know, when summer comes everyone understands that it is time to think about traveling to San Luis Potosí to enjoy the Potosina National Fair (FENAPO).
